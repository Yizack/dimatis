About

Eliézer Yizack Rangel Baysa, also known as Dimatis. He was born and raised in La Chorrera, Panama on June 15th, 1998. During 2013 he made some songs just for fun, but on August 24th, 2014, he started uploading his music on SoundCloud.

He loves the melodic electronic music scene, his main genre is Melodic Dubstep, but he has also adopted other genres like Chillstep, Future Garage, Chill, Liquid Drum & Bass, and Future Bass.

Over the years, Yizack has been improving the quality of his music and has been supported by important YouTube channels like AirwaveMusicTV, OnlyChillstep, La Musique Libre, MixHound, HearWeGo and has been in love with those communities.

Yizack is a melodic electronic music producer in Panama, his mission is to share his music with the world by providing beautiful emotions through the melodies of his songs and one day be recognized for his music in his country.

Some artists who inspired him were: Au5, Blackmill, Chime, Collin McLoughlin, Crystal Skies, ILLENIUM, Miles Away, Said the Sky, Seven Lions, Skrux, T-Mass, Tim Schaufert, Xilent, and many others.
