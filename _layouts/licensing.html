Click to open a guide to dispute RouteNote's Claim
  1. Make sure you have credited me in your video description.
  2. Click details of the copyright claim.
  3. Select action.
  4. Click dispute.
  5. Mark the "My dispute isn't based on any of the reasons above." checkbox and click Continue.
  6. Select the option "License" and click Continue.
  7. Mark the "I have permission to use the content from the copyright owner" checkbox.
  8. Write in the form "I have permission to use this song as stated on the artist's website https://dimatis.yizack.com/licensing", check the 3 checkboxes below the form and write your name in the signature field.
  9. The claim will be released by RouteNote within 3 days or maximum in a week.

Note: Music copyright claims are not the same as "strikes". A claim is often an automated process by music distribuitors to prevent a material from being used without proper credits. Having a video with copyright claim won't risk your channel.
