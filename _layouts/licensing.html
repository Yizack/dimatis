Guide to dispoet RouteNote's Claim
  1. Make sure you have credited me in your video description.
  2. Click details of the copyright claim.
  3. Select action.
  4. Click dispute.
  5. Mark the "My dispute isn't based on any of the reasons above." check box and click Continue.
  6. Select the option "License" and click Continue.
  7. Write in the form "I have permission to use this song as stated on the artists website https://dimatis.yizack.com/licensing".
